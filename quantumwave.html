QuantumWave

I initially began my journey on Bandcamp with a pretty terrible page. With equally terrible music.

Eventually I fragmented my page into two new pages: QuantumWave and QuantumKnots. Wave would be for the synthwave and generally poppier music, while Knots would focus mostly on ambient music. Clearly, one of these ended up winning!

QuantumWave has been the landing page for all of my music since about 2015, holding what I consider my more polished releases that have been tweaked well enough to be ready for streaming/purchase. As of writing, there are 31 releases on QuantumWave and two currently pending finalization.

If you would like to take a listen to some of my favorites, have a look at these!
